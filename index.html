Welcome to McKinnon Earthmoving and
McKinnon Environmental

McKinnon Earthmoving (Qld) Pty Ltd and McKinnon Environmental Pty Ltd (McKinnon) provides experienced, reliable and cost effective earthworks and environmental contracting services to industry. We are experts in Underground Storage Tank (UST) removal, demolition, environmental earthworks and groundwater and vapour remediation.

At McKinnon, we have an extensive range of remediation, wastewater treatment and earthworks equipment available for hire. We offer trained, competent and qualified technical and professional personnel to achieve your project goals.

We have over 25 years in operation and robust Work Health and Safety (WHS) and Quality management systems in place.
